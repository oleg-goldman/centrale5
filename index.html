      About the project

      Brokers say
      Project of studios and apartments
      Vincitore Aqua Dimore at Dubai Science Park is a project from Vincitore Real Estate featuring luxury studios, one and two bedroom apartments, and two and three bedroom aqua villas with designer private pools.
      Maria
      Real estate advisor
      15 minutes to the central spots
      Located at the main address of Dubai Science Park, the complex is part of a thriving and vibrant community. It has excellent connectivity to renowned business districts, shopping centres, educational, health and recreational facilities. The Vincitore Aqua Dimore residential complex is located in a quiet area of Dubai with the ready-made infrastructure of Dubai Science Park. The city center, Business Bay and Downtown Dubai are 15-20 minutes away.
      Sultan
      Real estate advisor
      Area with developed infrastructure
      Dubai Science Park is a dedicated business district located in Al Barsha South 2, which was developed to facilitate the growth of small and medium-sized companies in the science sector. The area is represented by both commercial and residential real estate, including apartments and villas. The infrastructure is very diverse – parking lots, shops, fitness studios, schools with a British curriculum and medical institutions such as the Genesis Healthcare Centre.
      Milana
      Real estate advisor
